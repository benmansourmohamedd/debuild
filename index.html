Research Method

deBUILD documents architecture as infrastructure — examining how spatial systems structure governance, access, and everyday survival. The archive prioritizes repeatable observation, standardized entries, and geographic specificity rather than design authorship.

Each entry functions as a structured research object combining field notes, diagrammatic analysis, and references.
